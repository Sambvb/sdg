1) Smart Public Transport Systems

SDG Alignment: SDG 11.2 – Access to Safe, Affordable, and Sustainable Transport Systems

This use case uses smart technology to create efficient, accessible, and eco-friendly public transport. It reduces traffic and pollution by optimizing routes with shortest path algorithms, graph analysis, and AI predictions. Real-time tracking, electric vehicles, and smart scheduling make travel easier, safer, and more sustainable for everyone.

2) Smart Waste Management Systems

SDG Alignment: SDG 11.6 – Reduce Environmental Impact

This use case uses smart technology to create efficient and sustainable waste management. It reduces pollution and improves recycling by planning waste collection routes with smart algorithms like shortest path, genetic algorithms, and clustering techniques. Real-time tracking, smart bins, and flexible scheduling make waste collection faster, cleaner, and better for the environment.

3) Smart Water Management Systems

SDG Alignment: SDG 6 – Clean Water and Sanitation

This use case uses smart technology to manage water resources efficiently, ensure clean water access, and minimize waste. It plans the best water distribution routes with Dijkstra's and Kruskal's algorithms. Real-time tracking, sensor networks, and smart scheduling help improve water supply, make distribution fair, and protect the environment.
